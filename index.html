Jia Xu

Ph.D. Candidate in Computational Medicine · Northeastern University

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computational Medicine at Northeastern University (ABD; expected graduation June 2027). My research develops trustworthy, human-centered artificial intelligence for healthcare: integrating multimodal clinical data (electronic health records, medical imaging, clinical notes, and wearable sensor data) with large language models, with applications in mental health, disease detection, and precision medicine. Previously, I was a graduate researcher at Shen Lab, Department of Biostatistics, Epidemiology and Informatics, University of Pennsylvania, advised by Dr. Li Shen.
