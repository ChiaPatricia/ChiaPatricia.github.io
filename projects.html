💬 Natural Language Processing & Large Language Models

Benchmark construction, instruction fine-tuning, and rigorous evaluation of large language models, with a focus on conversational mental-health support.

💬

MentalChat16K

A benchmark dataset of 16K counseling conversations that combines GPT-generated multi-turn dialogues across 33 mental-health topics with question-answer pairs drawn from real behavioral-intervention interviews, built for training and evaluating conversational mental-health models. Open models fine-tuned on it (LLaMA-2, Mistral, Vicuna, Zephyr) were assessed by both human experts and LLM judges across seven dimensions; it is now among the most-downloaded mental-health datasets on Hugging Face.

Hugging Face Paper
🧠

MentalGPT

A system of LLMs fine-tuned on MentalChat16K that outperforms base models and baselines on 7 mental-health metrics, evaluated by human experts alongside LLM judges (GPT-3.5, Gemini Pro).
